Corporate Social Responsibility Policy

Operating in a responsible and sustainable manner is important to The Hawk Aeronautics. While we run our business in line with the expectations of diverse stakeholders, we also see corporate responsibility as a discipline that helps us manage risks and maximize on the opportunities available to us in a changing world.

Our Corporate Governance Policies are intended to provide a framework for the governance of The Hawk Aeronautics and adherence to our policies and related operating procedures is the responsibility of every The Hawk Aeronautics Director, Officer and Employee.

We are committed to understanding, monitoring and managing our social, environmental and economic impact to enable us to contribute to our society wider goal of sustainable and valuable development. This commitment is deeply ingrained in our core values and we aim to demonstrate these responsibilities through our actions and within our corporate liabilities and policies


Job Scope

The post holder works within the Education Section or our youth society and works closely with The Hawk Aeronautics network of regionally based staff who recruit, manage and support volunteers at a local level. The post holder also liaises with colleagues across the organization to ensure our national resourcing and support of volunteers is coherent and reflects The Hawk Aeronautics broader mission.

In alignment with our Statement on Business Conduct and Ethics, Hawk will ensure that all matters of Corporate Social Responsibility are considered and supported in our operations and administrative matters and are consistent with Hawk stakeholders best interests. Hawk is committed to being recognized as a leader in the field of Corporate Social Responsibility and recognizes that in doing so, we will add significant value for our shareholders. This Policy applies to activities undertaken by or on behalf of The Hawk Aeronautics.

All Hawk employees, suppliers and contractors are to adopt the Corporate Social Responsibility considerations described in this policy into their day-to-day work activities. Hawk leaders are to act as role models by incorporating these considerations into decision-making in all business activities. The Hawk Aeronautics leaders are to ensure that appropriate organizational structures are in place to effectively identify, monitor, and manage Corporate Social Responsibility issues and performance relevant to our business.

Environment Health & Safety

The Hawk Aeronautics is committed to protecting the health and safety of all individuals affected by our activities, including our employees, contractors and the public. The Hawk Aeronautics will provide a safe and healthy working environment and will not compromise the health and safety of any individual.

The Hawk Aeronautics is committed to environmental protection and stewardship. The Hawk Aeronautics recognizes that pollution prevention, biodiversity and resource conservation are key to a sustainable environment and will effectively integrate these concepts into its business decision-making.

All employees and contractors are responsible and accountable for contributing to a safe working environment, for fostering safe working attitudes, and for operating in an environmentally responsible manner.

Community Involvement

The Hawk Aeronautics strives to understand and respect the cultural values and laws wherever we operate. We actively support initiatives in those communities where our employees live and work.

This commitment is visible in our contributions of financial, equipment and volunteer support in our society. We always encourage our employees to contribute time and energy in leadership and other roles in community organizations

BUSINESS ETHICS & TRANSPARENCY

Hawk is committed to maintaining the highest standards of integrity and corporate governance practices in order to maintain excellence in its daily operations, and to promote confidence in its governance systems. Hawk will conduct its business in an open, honest, and ethical manner. Hawk recognizes the importance of protecting all of our human, financial, physical, informational, social, environmental, and reputational assets. Hawk will advise its partners, contractors, and suppliers of its Corporate Social Responsibility Policy, and will work with them to achieve consistency with this policy. Hawk is committed to measuring, auditing and tracking the performance of its Corporate Social Responsibility programs.



STAKEHOLDER RELATIONS

As a global supplier to customers worldwide, The Hawk Aeronautics operates across a diverse range of cultures and international markets. We are proud of our commitment to apply fair labor practices, while respecting the national, International and local laws of the countries and communities where we operate and are committed to providing equal opportunity in all aspects of our employment. We do not engage in or tolerate unlawful workplace conduct, including harassment, discrimination or intimidation.
